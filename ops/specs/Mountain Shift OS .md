Serve as the core operating system and intelligence layer that powers:
1. Asset origination
2. Financial structuring
3. Real-time infrastructure management
4. Turning physical systems into programmable financial infrastructure

<u>Entity Type:</u>
1. Software & Systems Company
2. Domains:

    2. AI Research

<u>Core Positioning:</u>
Mountain Shift is not just a technology layer. It is an operating system for infrastructure, capital, and intelligence.

Mountain Shift helps:
1. Originate and evaluate assets
2. Monitor and optimize operations
3. Generate structured data for underwriting, risk, and capital allocation
4. Create repeatable workflows for infrastructure deployment and financial scaling
